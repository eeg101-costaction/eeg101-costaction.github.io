## 13 April 2026 - first EEG101 WG2 virtual meeting

🕑 15:00 – 17:00 (Central European Time)

📍 Online (Virtual Event)

This first EEG101 WG2 virtual meeting brings together researchers and practitioners working on EEG data curation, preprocessing, harmonization, and open data infrastructures. The symposium aims to foster discussion, share ongoing work, and build alignment around best practices within the EEG101 COST Action. The program and signup link will be posted here soon, and you will receive information over the online location by email.

## 24 September 2025 - MC1 meeting

The first management committee (MC) meeting was hosted on 24 September 2025 at the COST headquarters in Brussels. The COST action was explained to all members by Wioleta Walentowska (Science Officer) and Flavya Ricci (Administrative Officer), including the networking tools and financial support. We discussed the initial actions to be undertaken by the working groups. Furthermore, the action chair, vice chair and others of the core team were selected and voted into position.

## 20 May 2025 - EEG101 approved

We are happy that our proposal [Fundamentals of Open & Rigorous EEG Science (EEG101)](https://www.cost.eu/actions/CA24148) was approved by the COST Committee of Senior Officials.
