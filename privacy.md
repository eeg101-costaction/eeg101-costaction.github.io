See the [GitHub Privacy Statement][https://help.github.com/en/github/site-policy github-privacy-statement] for details.

## Website Analytics

We are committed to ensuring the privacy and anonymity of the visitors to this website. We use a self-hosted [Plausible](https://plausible.io) server to collect anonymous usage statistics and to count the number of visitors. No personal data is collected or tracked.
